About the Event

Night of Champions honors athletes and coaches for more than just their athletic ability. It lifts up important personal values such as character, integrity, and faith while honoring heroes that are true role models in the world of sports. This event provides an opportunity for executives, clients, and friends to “get up close and personal” with some of the greatest sports legends.
